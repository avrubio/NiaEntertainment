Welcome to Nia’s Collective,
the vibrant heart of Nia Entertainment!

At Nia’s Collective, we bring together a diverse community of exceptionally talented individuals, each passionate about their craft and eager to contribute to something greater than themselves.

Meet the Members of Nia's Collective!

Tniyah

Introducing Tniyah, the soulful R&B sensation from New Jersey, whose seductive voice channels the spirit of legends like Aaliyah and Beyoncé. As a member of Nia’s Collective, Tniyah brings her raw passion and vocal prowess to our diverse community, inspiring collaboration and creativity. Join us in celebrating Tniyah's talent as we continue to push the boundaries of music and community spirit together.

Welcome to the Collective, Tniyah!

Allahna

Introducing Allahna, a multi-talented artist hailing from the vibrant streets of New Jersey. With a diverse portfolio spanning from mesmerizing paintings to intricate pen portraits and captivating album cover art, Allahna is a true visionary. As a member of Nia’s Collective, Allahna enriches our community with her boundless creativity and unique artistic perspective. Her ability to breathe life into her creations resonates with the core values of our collective, where talent meets community and innovation knows no bounds.

Welcome to the Collective, Allahna!

Sidney

Introducing Sidney, the adventurous lifestyle blogger hailing from Jersey and currently embracing the stunning landscapes of Arizona. With a passion for travel and a keen eye for capturing life's moments, Sidney shares her experiences and insights through her engaging blog. As a member of Nia’s Collective, Sidney brings a unique perspective on exploration, culture, and the beauty of everyday life. Join us in following Sidney's journey as she continues to inspire us with her wanderlust and zest for life.

Welcome to the Collective, Sidney!
