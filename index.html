Meet our Members of Nia's Collective

We’re a dynamic group of individuals who are passionate about what we do and dedicated to delivering our authentic selves!

Tniyah

Introducing Tniyah, the soulful R&B sensation from New Jersey, whose seductive voice channels the spirit of legends like Aaliyah and Beyoncé. As an emerging artist in R&B, Tniyah is signed to the forefront of the music industry, poised to make waves with her undeniable talent and magnetic presence.
As a member of Nia’s Collective, Tniyah brings her raw passion and vocal prowess to our diverse community, inspiring collaboration and creativity. Her artistry transcends boundaries, inviting listeners into a world where emotion flows freely and music becomes a transformative experience. Join us in celebrating Tniyah's talent as we continue to push the boundaries of music and community spirit together.

Welcome to the Collective, Tniyah!

Allahna

Introducing Allahna, a multi-talented artist hailing from the vibrant streets of New Jersey. With a diverse portfolio spanning from mesmerizing paintings to intricate pen portraits and captivating album cover art, Allahna is a true visionary.
As a member of Nia’s Collective, Allahna enriches our community with her boundless creativity and unique artistic perspective. Her ability to breathe life into her creations resonates with the core values of our collective, where talent meets community and innovation knows no bounds.

Welcome to the Collective, Allahna!

Sidney

Introducing Sidney, the adventurous lifestyle blogger hailing from Jersey and currently embracing the stunning landscapes of Arizona. With a passion for travel and a keen eye for capturing life's moments, Sidney shares her experiences and insights through her engaging blog.
As a member of Nia’s Collective, Sidney brings more than just picturesque scenery to the table. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for storytelling, she shares her experiences and insights through her captivating blog, offering readers a glimpse into the beauty of everyday life and the richness of diverse cultures.

Welcome to the Collective, Sidney!
